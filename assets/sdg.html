About the
SDG Action Labs

As we approach 2030, the urgency to foster an environment conducive to transformative social enterprises, equitable resource mobilisation, and locally-led development has never been more evident. The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) demand our collective action for lasting impact. Enter the SDG Action Labs, a visionary platform that shapes the narratives for change.

The
Journey Ahead

The event—a collaborative convergence of dynamic minds—is set to unfold in Bengaluru, Karnataka. A multi-stakeholder symposium that marries experiential learning with forward-looking discussions, the SDG Action Labs seeks to redefine the trajectory of sustainable development.

SDG Action Labs

Each SDG would ideally be mapped to a group of interested members, with a chair appointed among them to preside over a Working Group. The members of each Working Group would be responsible for developing its agenda for the conclave. Each group would be advised to convene periodically prior to the event to set out its objectives and approach to achieving them in the form of a Solution Circle.

SDG Action Labs The Endgame

Solving intractable problems which continue to challenge us (including taking tested solutions to scale)

  • To bring together multi-dimensional expertise of doers, thinkers and funders
  • To test and scale solutions
  • To move beyond learning: focus on end use

An organised group of people who:

Hold a common interest in a specific thematic or service domain

Are Committed to collective learning, collaboration and advancing the knowledge and practice of the domain

Demonstrate self-organising systems - people are the Solution Circle

Have an Endgame in Mind towards solving a problem (test an idea, design a model, generate evidence)

Curated Themes;

Theme and Cross-theme Engagement Designed

Innovative Financing

What are new financial instruments (including results and outcome-based), how do they compare with each other, how do we measure their efficiency and effectiveness from a result and community perspective, and how do we improve reporting on returns? What is the future?

Transformation for Growth

How to manage large-scale transformation—of processes, organisations, and systems, what investments are required, how to manage various stakeholder interests, and how to bring rigour to change. What is the future?

Tech in Social Development

Technology types, where is it applied, where can maximum returns emerge, what kinds of technology are working, how do we know what is making a difference, and what is stopping collaboration? What is the future?

Social Behaviour Change

What theories and practices work in changing social norms and individual behaviour, how do we know they work, how do we scale them, and what investments are required? What is the future?

Impact at Scale

Build on learning and unlock the vast present and future potential to find efficient alternatives and strategies to enable impact at scale. Understand scale definition, metrics, solutions, and policies that can assist in unlocking impact and providing proof of impact at scale.
